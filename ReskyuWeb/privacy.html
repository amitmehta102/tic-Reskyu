Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 2025

🔒 Summary: RESKYU collects only the data needed to run the platform — your email, name, and role. We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and database. We do not sell your data. Ever.

1. Who We Are

RESKYU is a food surplus rescue marketplace connecting restaurants with surplus food to students, consumers, and NGOs at discounted prices. We are operated by the RESKYU team and can be reached at hello@reskyu.in.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you create an account or use RESKYU:

  • Account data: Name, email address, and role (Restaurant / NGO / Consumer) that you provide during signup.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, buttons clicked, and features used — collected via Google Analytics (anonymized).
  • Listing data: Food items, prices, and pickup times listed by restaurants.
  • Claim data: Pickups scheduled by NGOs, including item name and quantity.
  • Waitlist data: Email address if you join the waitlist.
  • Contact data: Name, email, and message if you submit our contact form.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To show your role-specific dashboard (restaurant listings, NGO browse, etc.)
  • To connect restaurants with nearby students and NGOs
  • To calculate and display your impact stats (meals rescued, CO₂ saved)
  • To send password reset emails when you request them
  • To improve the platform using aggregated, anonymous analytics
  • To respond to contact and partnership enquiries

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to power RESKYU:

  • Firebase (Google LLC): Authentication and Firestore database. Your data is stored on Google's servers under their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics 4: Website usage analytics with IP anonymization enabled. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Netlify: Website hosting. Netlify may log server access logs per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts: Typography. Google may log font request metadata.

5. Firebase API Keys

Our Firebase configuration (API keys) is visible in the client-side source code. This is intentional and safe — Firebase API keys are not secret credentials. Access to your data is controlled by Firebase Security Rules, not by API key secrecy. This is documented by Google's own Firebase guidelines.

6. Data Storage & Security

  • All data is stored in Google Firestore (Mumbai region, ap-south-1).
  • Authentication is handled by Firebase Auth with industry-standard encryption.
  • Passwords are hashed by Firebase — we never see your raw password.
  • Dashboard pages are marked noindex to prevent search engines from indexing private data.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data via your profile page
  • Delete your account and all associated data by emailing us
  • Opt out of analytics by using the Google Analytics opt-out tool

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@reskyu.in.

8. Children's Privacy

RESKYU is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above.
