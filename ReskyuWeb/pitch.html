The Problem

"40% of restaurant food
goes to waste — every single day."

India's restaurant sector produces over 9.4 million tonnes of food waste annually. Surplus is prepared daily yet discarded before close — a systemic failure affecting merchants, communities, and the planet.

No platform today offers a zero-logistics, AI-assisted bridge between surplus food and the millions who need it within walking distance. The food expires. The opportunity vanishes. The waste compounds.

The Solution

One tap.
Surplus rescued.

RESKYU turns end-of-day surplus into instant opportunity — no trucks, no drivers, no waste.

🚶
Zero-Logistics Model
Buyers walk to the restaurant. No delivery riders, no packaging overhead, no emissions. Pickup within a walkable radius keeps the model lean and carbon-zero.
🤖
SurplusIQ AI
Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash, SurplusIQ predicts daily surplus quantities based on order history, day-of-week patterns, and weather — before the restaurant even closes.
🔀
Dual-Channel Routing
Students and consumers get surplus at up to 60% off. Registered NGOs get remaining inventory for free via volume routing — every meal finds a home.
How It Works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

01
AI Predicts, Merchant Confirms
SurplusIQ generates a surplus prediction. The merchant reviews and publishes in a single tap — live in under 10 seconds.
→
02
Geofence Notifies Nearby
Consumers and NGOs within walking radius receive instant push notifications via FCM — real-time, location-aware, prioritised by proximity.
→
03
Pay, Pick Up, Done
Consumer pays via UPI in-app upfront. No-show protection guards the merchant. They walk in, collect, leave. Zero delivery. Full rescue.
Key Features

Built for
real-world rescue.

🧠
SurplusIQ Prediction
Gemini-powered daily surplus forecasting based on sales history, patterns, and contextual signals.
⏱️
Dynamic Time-Fencing
Listings auto-expire at restaurant close time. No stale food, no missed windows, no confusion.
🔒
Atomic No-Double-Booking
Firestore transactions guarantee inventory atomicity — two users can never claim the same portion.
🏢
NGO Volume Routing
Verified NGOs receive bulk surplus allocation automatically when consumer demand doesn't clear inventory.
📊
ESG Trust Score
Every restaurant gets a live ESG score — meals rescued, CO₂ saved, community contributions. Fully auditable.
🛡️
Upfront UPI No-Show Shield
Payment captured at booking via Razorpay. Merchants are protected from no-shows with automatic refund logic.
