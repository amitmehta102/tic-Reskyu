The Problem

A crisis hiding in plain sight
— every single day.

India's restaurants prepare food for peak demand — but demand is unpredictable. Every evening, thousands of kitchens discard perfectly edible surplus because there is no efficient way to redistribute it before close.

The result is a paradox: restaurants absorb the loss, surplus food rots in bins, and meanwhile millions of people within walking distance go hungry or pay full price for a meal they can barely afford.

Existing solutions require delivery infrastructure, cold chains, or costly logistics — making them impractical for the daily, time-sensitive nature of restaurant surplus. No platform has cracked the zero-logistics model. Until now.

How It Works

One tap. Surplus rescued.

RESKYU removes every point of friction between surplus food and the people nearby who want it.

01
Merchant Lists Surplus
Our AI analyses order history and suggests today's likely surplus. The restaurant confirms in a single tap — the listing goes live in under 10 seconds, with price, quantity, and pickup window.
→
02
Nearby Users Get Notified
Consumers and NGOs within walking radius receive an instant push notification. Real-time, location-aware, prioritised by proximity — no browsing required.
→
03
Pay, Walk In, Pick Up
The consumer pays upfront in-app. They walk to the restaurant, collect their order, and leave. Zero delivery. Zero packaging. Full rescue — for people and for the planet.
Features

Built for real-world rescue.

Every feature is designed around the actual constraints of restaurant surplus — speed, trust, and zero logistics overhead.

🧠
AI Surplus Prediction
Analyses your restaurant's sales history and patterns to forecast daily surplus quantities before close — so you list faster and waste less.
⏱️
Dynamic Time-Fencing
Listings automatically expire at your restaurant's close time. No stale offers, no confusion — every listing is live, fresh, and time-bound.
🔒
Guaranteed No-Double-Booking
Atomic transactions ensure inventory integrity — two buyers can never claim the same portion. Every order is confirmed or rejected instantly.
🏢
NGO Volume Routing
When consumer demand doesn't clear all surplus, verified NGOs are automatically notified and can claim remaining inventory in bulk — always free.
📊
ESG Trust Score
Every restaurant receives a live sustainability score — meals rescued, CO₂ avoided, community impact. Fully transparent and shareable.
🛡️
No-Show Protection
Payment is captured upfront. If a buyer doesn't show, the merchant is protected automatically — no chasing, no disputes, no lost revenue.
