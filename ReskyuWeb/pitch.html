RESKYU is a social impact marketplace on a mission to eliminate food waste by connecting surplus meals from restaurants directly to the people nearby who need them most.

India wastes enough food to feed millions — yet millions go hungry every day. Our platform bridges that gap with zero logistics and zero waste.

How It Works

STEP ONE

Our AI analyses your restaurant's order history and predicts today's surplus before close. Confirm in a single tap — listed in seconds with price, quantity, and pickup window.

STEP TWO

Consumers and NGOs within walking distance receive an instant push notification. Real-time, location-aware, prioritised by proximity — no browsing required.

STEP THREE

Pay upfront in-app, walk to the restaurant, collect your order and leave. Zero delivery. Zero packaging waste. Full rescue — for people and for the planet.
